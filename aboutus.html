About Us

Objective

Our objective is to preserve and celebrate Rendang Maman, a traditional dish from Negeri Sembilan, by documenting its history, and cultural significance. We aim to raise awareness and appreciation for this iconic dish, ensuring its legacy is passed on to future generations.

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to create a digital archive dedicated to Rendang Maman, highlighting its cultural value and history. We seek to promote this traditional dish globally, fostering pride in its heritage and sharing its story with a wider audience.
